Raphael

Raphael is the cool, caring uncle who unsuccessfully attempts to keep up with modern social trends. He has curly, wavy hair and wears a white shirt and jeans, suggesting a more casual and youthful image. He intends the best for Adam and Eve but depicts his stories as brilliant and showy, leading to some exaggerations and miscommunications regarding the content of his stories. He is still unafraid to display the truth and is open to deeper topics/questions with Adam and Eve.
