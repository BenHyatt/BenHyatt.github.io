Jesus

Jesus is the “cool and nice guy.” Or you could say, “The Humble All-Star.” He grew up self-disciplined and is head-strong like God yet more approachable and warm. He has wavy, pulled-back hair indicative of his more relaxed personality and is more familiar with modern day jargon and social customs. He will calmly reprimand you if you lie, cheat, or commit any other offense, is available and responsive, and will give you a fist bump. He wears smart, clean clothing just like God, but his appearance is a bit more relaxed and youthful: he has less facial hair and wears a polo and jeans as opposed to God’s more professional khakis and white button-up.
