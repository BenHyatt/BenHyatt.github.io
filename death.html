Death

Death takes the role of a mysterious Man in Black from popular conspiracy theories. Similar to these men, Death's outward appearance immediately inspires fear in those he meets, and not much is known about him. In fact, posting textual content would reveal too much about Death, so he limits himself to status updates about his voracious appetite, representative of his gluttonous nature.
