Adam

Adam is a man trying to make his way through a new, confusing world. After his wife, Eve, eats the apple, he becomes overwhelmed with emotions, ultimately resolving to follow his wife in sinning. Being the first man to come into existence, Adam is in a unique situation. However, he handles it in a very normal and seemingly straightforwardly tragic way. For instance, he processes it sort of akin to a Shakespearean Tragedy by lamenting the fall of Eve and his potential mistakes that may have led to it. While it would be easy to focus on the novelty of Adam’s situation, we decided to accentuate the universality of his life choices by depicting him as a common man. He wears business clothes as you’d expect from a nondescript office worker at a boring company—think Oscar or Dwight from The Office TV series.
