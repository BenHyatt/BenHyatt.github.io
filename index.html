Say hello to the GOLD Party.

The GOLD Party—short for the Greater Organization for Leadership in Democracy—is the only bona fide political party at the Upper Arlington High School. We run candidates for class elections, work to raise civic engagement, and advocate for student rights.

The party currently boasts around 80 members. Any UAHS student can become a member.

Party rules and procedures are outlined in the bylaws.
