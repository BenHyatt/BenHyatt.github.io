Policing in UA

The GOLD Party is working to gather data and information regarding the efficacy of School Resource Officers in Upper Arlington. This is not only in response to the horrific murder of George Floyd, but also in response to the recent examples of police officers attacking, gasing, tazing, arresting, and shooting innocent civilians and members of the press. To do this, we are taking advantage of Ohio Revised Code 149.43, which gives us, as citizens, the right to inspect documents from public institutions; this effort will culminate in an executive summary, which will include a policy recommendation. We are also seeking out the voices of students and community stakeholders. Suggestions should be sent to elt@UAGOLDParty.org.

Say hello to the GOLD Party.

The GOLD Party—short for the Greater Organization for Leadership in Democracy—is the only bona fide political party at the Upper Arlington High School. We run candidates for class elections, work to raise civic engagement, and advocate for student rights.

The party currently boasts around 80 members. Any UAHS student can become a member.

Party rules and procedures are outlined in the bylaws.
